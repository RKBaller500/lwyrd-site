# LWYRD Demo Video Script

**Format:** Screen recording + voiceover. Informal, direct.  
**Target length:** ~1.5–2.5 minutes  

---

## INTRO (0:00–0:15)

*(On the homepage)*

> "This is LWYRD — a matching platform that connects people with specialized law firms. Here's how it works."

---

## HOMEPAGE (0:15–0:45)

> "The homepage gives you the pitch and the process. Three steps: fill out a short intake, we find your match, and you get a ranked list of firms. Nobody contacts you until you make the first move."

*(Scroll quickly through the page)*

> "Everything here leads to one action — Get Matched."

---

## INTAKE FLOW (0:45–1:45)

*(Click "Get Matched," sign in, land on /intake/start)*

> "After signing in you hit a quick orientation screen — five minutes, plain questions, your answers are private. Then you're into the intake."

*(Click "Let's Get Started," enter the wizard)*

> "First thing — who are you? Startup, small business, or individual. That determines which questions you see."

*(Select an option, move through a few questions)*

> "The questions cover what kind of legal issue you have, how urgent it is, your budget, and what matters most to you in a firm. No legal jargon."

*(Hit submit)*

> "Submit, and we run the match."

---

## RESULTS (1:45–2:15)

*(Land on /results)*

> "You get a ranked list of matched firms with a fit score and a reason for each match. Click into any firm to see their full profile, and when you're ready, send an introduction. The firm gets your intake summary with it, so the first conversation is already informed."

---

## OUTRO (2:15–2:30)

> "That's it — intake, match, connect. Free for clients, and built around one idea: the right firm for your situation exists. LWYRD helps you find them."

---

## RECORDING NOTES

- Pre-fill auth before recording so you don't type live
- Move the cursor slowly, let each screen settle before speaking
- Skip the footer
